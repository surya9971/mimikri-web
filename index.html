How to Play

Three steps. Zero setup. Maximum hungama.

1
🎴

PICK A DECK

Bollywood, Cricket, Hit Hindi Songs… or mix your own combo with Mixed Play.

2
📱

FOREHEAD, PLEASE

Hold the phone to your forehead — everyone else acts, sings and describes the word.

3
🏆

TILT TO SCORE

Guessed it? Tilt down. Stuck? Tilt up to pass. Rack up points before the buzzer!

✓  Tilt down = Correct ✗  Tilt up = Pass
