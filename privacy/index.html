PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 12 July 2026

The short version: Mimikri has no accounts, no login, and collects no personal information. Your scores and settings live on your phone and nowhere else. That's the whole game. 🎬

1. Who we are

Mimikri ("we", "us") is a party game app for iOS and Android. This policy explains what information the app handles and — mostly — what it doesn't. It applies to the Mimikri app and this website (mimikri.in).

2. What we collect

Nothing personal. Mimikri does not ask for, collect, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Accounts, logins, or passwords
  • Your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location

The app works fully offline. There is no Mimikri server receiving your data, because there is no Mimikri server at all.

3. What stays on your device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your phone only:

  • Game settings (round timer, difficulty, sound and haptics preferences)
  • Recent round scores and per-deck best scores

This never leaves your device. Deleting the app deletes it all. You can also clear your score history any time from Settings inside the app.

4. Motion sensors

During a round, Mimikri reads your phone's motion sensor so you can tilt to mark a card correct or pass. Sensor readings are used in the moment and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

5. Advertising

Mimikri is free, supported by ads shown only between rounds — never during gameplay. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may process certain device information (such as an advertising identifier) to show and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Where required, you'll be asked for consent (for example, App Tracking Transparency on iOS), and you can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. We do not receive or store any of this information ourselves.

6. Children

Mimikri is a family-friendly party game. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Ads, where shown, are configured to be family-appropriate.

7. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Mimikri handles data (for example, adding an online feature), we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above before the change takes effect. The current promise stands: no accounts, offline-first, ads never mid-round.

8. Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@mimikri.in or via the contact page.
